Instructions

Reduce your opponent’s life total to 0 using Attacks to win! Block attacks to avoid damage! Throw your opponent to damage them if they’re blocking! And try not to get trapped in the corner...

Controls

Player 1 (left side)
Jump: w
Left: a
Right: d
Block: s
Light Attack: left shift
Heavy Attack: q
Throw: left shift & s (at the same time)

Player 2 (right side)
Jump: p
Left: l
Right: '
Block: ;
Light Attack: right shift
Heavy Attack: [
Throw: right shift & ; (at the same time)
